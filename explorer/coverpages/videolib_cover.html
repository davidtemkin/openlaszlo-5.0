Launch App

This application opens in a window. If you don't see it, you may need to bring that window to the front.

A small video player with a clip library: pick a clip from the list, then play, pause, and scrub it with the transport controls.

OpenLaszlo's stock videoview was Flash-only (built on Flash's NetStream/NetConnection). This demo plays through htmlvideoview, a drop-in replacement that wraps a browser-native HTML5 <video> element, so the same LZX application now streams video in DHTML with no plugin.
