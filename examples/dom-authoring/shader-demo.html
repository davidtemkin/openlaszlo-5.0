<shader> — domain-warped noise, sliders driving uniforms through ${…} constraints

The zoom and warp uniforms are constraint-bound to the sliders — drag and the GPU follows, no event code. speed changes the rate of an integrated phase attribute (phase += speed·dt), so the animation never jumps: multiplying time · speed in the shader would rescale accumulated time and snap the pattern on every change.

The visual is domain warping — technique by Inigo Quilez (see his Shadertoy examples 4s23zzM and lsl3RH) — implemented independently here over this distro's own simplex-noise port (McEwan/Gustavson, Ashima Arts) and his cosine palettes.
